About us

Welcome to Break__Shake!

The purpose of Break__shake is to document the food, food stories, culture, and tourism of different parts of India on a single platform. Also, share vegetarian recipes developed at home with people around the world.

The purpose of this blog is to encourage people to step out of their routines and enjoy their personal lives. This can be done by either exploring new places, trying new, tasty foods or cooking some scrumptious tasting treats in their own kitchen.
